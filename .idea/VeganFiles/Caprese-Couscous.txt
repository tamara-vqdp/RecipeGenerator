Caprese Couscous

Prep time: 10 mins
Cook time: 5 mins

Ingredients:
1 cup whole wheat couscous, dry
1 cup water
3 roma tomatoes, diced
1 cup chickpeas, drained and rinsed
1 medium avocado, diced
1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped

For the dressing:
2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
squeeze of half a lemon
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
salt and pepper

Directions
1. Boil 1 cup of water on the stove. Add dry couscous, stir, cover, and remove from heat. 
Let sit for 3-5 minutes, until couscous is puffy and water is absorbed.

2. Transfer cooked couscous to a large bowl and add chickpeas, diced tomato, and salt and pepper. 
Stir to combined. Whisk together you balsamic dressing and pour over the salad. Toss to coat.

3. Add the fresh chopped basil and avocado to the salad. Gently stir to combine. Add any additional salt and 
pepper needed, then serve it on up!
